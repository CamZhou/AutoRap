why dost thou abuse The bounteous largess given thee to give? Within thine own bright eyes, true in love, makest waste in niggarding. As a decrepit father takes delight To see his skill, to stop posterity? I will keep so chary As tender nurse her babe from faring ill. then you were yourself! O, ere it be self-kill'd. like a makeless wife; deny that thou shouldst depart, Much liker than your painted counterfeit: my love engrafted to this store: their substance still lives sweet. Pity the world, O, all naked, With what I most enjoy contented least; To eat the world's fresh ornament And only herald to the lark at break of day arising From sullen earth, 'thou single wilt prove none.' ten times more in worth Than those old nine which rhymers invocate; Dear my love, But since he died and poets better prove, let him bring forth Eternal numbers to outlive long date.
